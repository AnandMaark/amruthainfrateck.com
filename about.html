About Amrutha Infratech

M/s Amrutha Infratech service is a provider of end to end constructions material & Building Restoration services and solutions to help clients to improve safety and longevity of structures. We have been relentlessly working on providing services and solutions in the area of concrete repairs, all type of waterproofing works and industrial painting and Flooring & Special grouts system works.

The company is specialized in delivering services and solutions to help clients. We ought to be acquainted with respect to concrete structural repairs &all type of waterproofing i.e., cause of distress, assessment & extend the service life.

The Owner of Amrutha Infratech is Mr. Chandra Sekhar, he is highly Qualified in his field of expertise

The management of Amrutha infratech strongly relies on all employees to achieve the set targets and goals in the business and the corporate environment so that they will be able to be successful in this industry

Our highly trained and experienced technical personnel are supported by the planning and administration section to ensure that there is follow-through and monitoring of all projects from appointment through to completion and handover.

Quality, efficient service ad execution as well as customer satisfaction are our guidelines in conducting our business. We also emphasis public and occupational safety and health during project implementation.

Amrutha Infratech service Company Background

M/s Amrutha Infratech service was registered in 2017 as a Construction Solution material distributor and applicator based in Reputed Construction Chemical Companies Gauteng Province .The firm was a vision of a Construction practitioner with a passion for the building Waterproofing , grouts and concrete repairs with the support of the waterproofing product and paint manufactures , the company came into fruition and has found a nice in the construction and facilities management industries.

Our target market includes industrial ,government departments, private developers, general contractors ,private property owners and investors. We have already successfully carried out various projects for Institution/ Departments

Specialist in Water Proofing

As Specialist in Water Proofing distributor and Applicator, We Endeavour to provide total one stop service in new construction of new works, maintenanceof existing infrastructure,as well as improvement/additions to or alterations of infrastructure.
